## Root Cause (bugs only)
Milestone 6 added a `write_plan_state()` call to `run_plan_interview()` (line 69 of `stages/plan_interview.sh`), but the test subprocess in `test_plan_interview_stage.sh` did not source `lib/plan_state.sh` or export `PLAN_STATE_FILE`. This caused `write_plan_state` to fail as an undefined function under `set -euo pipefail`, killing the subprocess before "Session End" was written to the log. Additionally, `set -e` from the sourced `plan_interview.sh` prevented non-zero exit codes from being captured by `echo $?`.
